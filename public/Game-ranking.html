The Best GAMES According to Ume

My top 3 Games of all-time.

TES: Skyrim

This is my favourite RPG. This will immerse you in the world of Skyrim in Tamriel; to seek fame and defeat dragons. DRAGONS!

Grimdawn

As the title of the game, it is grim. You were possessed and survived after a near-death experienced from being hanged. You realized after waking up that you are not your old self but a human that got superpowers from the arcane. Interested? Grimdawn is an ARPG game and what hooked me here beside the lore is the massive customization and build.

Terraria

It has wings and is better than Minecraft.
